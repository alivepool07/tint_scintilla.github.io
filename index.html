About SCINTILLA

Welcome to our website

We, TINTians are organising SCINTILLA, the science and linguistic exhibition under the Department of BASIC SCIENCE AND HUMANITIES. A call out to science enthusiasts and laudable linguists to present Models, Prototypes and Content driving cognitive communion that tantalizes your imagination and piques your curiosity. So, SCINTILLA 2022 welcomes all to participate. Scroll down the page for further details and registration link. Hoping to see you soon.

Poster

Intersting , illustrative , stimulating and informative posters are created by YOU and are welcomed.

Prototype

Inovative ,out of the box ,environment and user-friendly prototypes and models are invited.
